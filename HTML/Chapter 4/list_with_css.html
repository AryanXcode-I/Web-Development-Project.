Steps To Bake A Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Grease and flour a baking pan.
  3. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. In another bowl, beat sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Stir in vanilla essence for flavor.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  8. Pour in milk and mix until the batter is smooth.
  9. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  10. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  11. Let the cake cool before removing it from the pan.
  12. Decorate or frost as desired. Enjoy your cake!
